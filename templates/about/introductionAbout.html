About Son & Comp

Sun & Company is a leading manpower placement agency based in Kathmandu, Nepal, with over two decades of experience in connecting skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled workers with international employers. We are dedicated to offering top-notch recruitment solutions that meet the specific needs of employers from various industries across the globe, including construction, hospitality, healthcare, and more.
Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our stringent worker selection process, timely deployment, and unwavering focus on worker performance and medical fitness. We take pride in our excellent track record and our ability to promptly provide high-quality service, ensuring that both employers and workers have a seamless experience.
Through strategic partnerships and a deep understanding of the global job market, we have successfully placed thousands of Nepali workers in countries such as the Gulf States, Malaysia, Singapore, and beyond. Our recruitment process covers everything from worker identification and CV preparation to medical checks, orientation, and deployment.
